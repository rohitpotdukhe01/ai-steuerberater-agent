SteuerPilot

An AI-agent system that helps people in Germany prepare their tax return with confidence, clarity, and a complete deduction checklist.

Why people use it
  • Agent-guided intake that finds missing deductions
  • Evidence-first checklists to maximize refund readiness
  • Transparent preparation steps without submitting your tax return

Difference vs Taxfix

Taxfix focuses on submission through ELSTER. SteuerPilot stays in preparation mode: it helps you gather every relevant detail, create a deduction playbook, and hand over a clean summary you can file yourself or with a tax advisor.

Difference vs ELSTER

ELSTER is the official filing portal. SteuerPilot is an AI co-pilot that organizes your information, highlights overlooked categories, and builds a document checklist so your ELSTER filing is faster and less error-prone.

Core differentiators

  • Multi-agent flow: intake → deduction scout → document coach → summary
  • Privacy-first: no submission, no credential collection
  • Explainable: every suggestion comes with questions and evidence needed

How the agent system works

1. Capture profile and income context
2. Scan every deduction category
3. Build a document checklist
4. Produce a prep-ready summary
